AREPR is a disaster relief organization based in Pureto Rico and in partnership with Michigan State University, hoped to redesign a website that would be more intuitive and accessible for users.

In this project, I collaborated with other students to create a style guide, website redesign, navigation redesign, and provide a new identity for AREPR.

In the process, we researched deeply about AREPR and ran content audits on other disaster relief organizations' websites to get a sense of what like users would typically need access to. We used wireframes to develop a design and prototyped a website on Adobe that would be most beneficial to AREPR users.
